Atal Bihari Vajpayee- Bhishma Pitamah of Indian Politics

1924-2018

Co-founder and a Senior leader of BJP

A Great Leader and a Renowned Poet of India

☛ Atal Bihari Vajpayee was an Indian politician and statesman who served three terms as the 10th Prime minister of India.

☛ He was the member of the Indian Parliament for over five decades, having been elected ten times to the Lok Sabha and twice to the Rajya Sabha.

☛ His activism started in Gwalior with Arya Kumar Sabha,the youth wing of the Arya Samaj movement, of which he bacame the general secretary in 1944.He also joined the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh(RSS) in 1939 as Swayamsevak or volunteers.

☛ By 1942, at the age of 16 years, he became an active member of the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh(RSS).

☛ In 1951, Vajpayee was seconded by the RSS, along with Deendayal Upadhyaya, to work for the newly formed Bharatiya Jana Sangh, a Hindu right-wing political party associated with the RSS.

☛ During his tenure as prime minister, India carried out the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. Vajpayee sought to improve diplomatic relations with Pakistan, travelling to Lahore by bus to meet with Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif.

☛ In 2015, he was conferred India's highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna, by the president of India, Pranab Mukherjee. Also received the Padma Vibhusan in the year 1992.
